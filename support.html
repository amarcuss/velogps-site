Frequently Asked Questions

Why do some values show "—"?

A dash indicates the sensor data is unavailable. This can happen when GPS hasn't acquired a fix yet, or on devices without a barometer. Give your device a moment outdoors with a clear view of the sky.

How do I keep GPS active on the Lock Screen?

Start a Live Activity by tapping the antenna icon in the toolbar. The app will ask for "Always" location permission so it can continue updating in the background.

Where is my data stored?

All data stays on your device. VeloGPS has no servers, no accounts, and no cloud sync. See our Privacy Policy for details.
