Privacy Policy

VeloGPS for iOS

No Data Collection

VeloGPS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information — including your location, speed, altitude, and bookmarks — is processed entirely on your device and never leaves it.

Location Data

VeloGPS uses your device's GPS to display your position, speed, and altitude. Location data is used solely for real-time display within the app and is not recorded, uploaded, or shared with anyone.

Motion & Barometer Data

VeloGPS accesses your device's barometer to display atmospheric pressure and relative altitude. This sensor data is processed on-device and is not collected or transmitted.

Bookmarks

Saved bookmarks are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. They are not synced to any server or shared with third parties.

No Analytics or Tracking

VeloGPS contains no analytics frameworks, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party code. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

No Accounts

VeloGPS does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-up, no login, and no way to identify you.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact support.
